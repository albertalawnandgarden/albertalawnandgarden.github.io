Welcome to Alberta Lawn and Garden, a landscaping company in St. Albert, Alberta.

Alberta Lawn and Garden was established in 2004 and we offer landscaping services for both commercial and residential properties in St. Albert, Edmonton, and surrounding areas.

We do commercial lawn maintenance and snow removal for condominium apartments, businesses, and acreages. Besides lawn maintenance we build decks and fences, patio and sidewalk blocks, and retaining walls. We specialize in beautifying your yard and reducing your yard maintenance workload by adding flowerbeds, patios, and rock-beds.

Eric Oosterhuis, the owner, has an enormous amount of experience in his field. Eric is a graduate from the University of Alberta's Agriculture faculty. Eric worked as a research agronomist with Alberta Agriculture for eight years promoting direct seeding techniques to producers across Alberta, he then worked as the Agricultural superintendent for Agrium Inc. before starting Albert Lawn and Garden. Eric is happy to work with you specific to your landscaping needs.

"Beautifying Alberta one yard at a time!"
